Common Questions

Everything You
Need to Know.

How long does a full roof replacement take? +

Most residential roof replacements are completed in 1–3 days depending on the size and complexity of the roof. We'll give you a precise timeline during your free estimate consultation.

Do you work with insurance companies? +

Yes. We are experienced insurance claim specialists. We'll document the damage, work directly with your adjuster, and handle the entire claim process so you get what you're owed with zero stress.

What roofing materials do you install? +

We install all major roofing materials: architectural asphalt shingles, designer shingles, metal roofing (standing seam and exposed fastener), slate, tile, TPO, and EPDM for commercial applications.

What does your warranty cover? +

We offer a 25-year workmanship warranty on all installations. This covers labor and installation defects. Materials also come with manufacturer warranties (typically 30–50 years) which we help you register.

Is the estimate really free? +

100% free, no obligation. One of our certified roofing specialists will inspect your roof, document any issues, and provide a detailed written estimate — at zero cost to you.
